Express Ventilation was founded in 1992 by David Renton. The company is based in central Wolverhampton, specialising in the manufacture and installation of ventilation systems.


In its 16 year history EVS has developed an outstanding reputation for high standards and lasting relationships, serving clients nationwide. We have experience in a varied range of environments, spreading from small to large contracts.


EVS takes an 'always learning' approach to work, keeping in touch with industry standards, whilst always been involved in innovative methods and advances in the industry. This is highlighted by our involvement in the SKILLcard initiative. This involves having all of our staff having recognised skills, competence and qualifications whilst also enhancing their health and safety awareness.
